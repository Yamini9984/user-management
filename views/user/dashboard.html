Frequently Asked Questions

What is the baggage policy?

Each passenger is allowed one carry-on bag and one personal item. Check with your airline for specific restrictions.

How do I change my flight?

You can change your flight by logging into your account and going to 'My Bookings'. Select the flight you want to change and follow the instructions.

What should I do if my flight is delayed?

If your flight is delayed, check your email for updates from your airline, and you can also monitor the flight status on our website.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support via email at support@flightbooking.com or call us at 1-800-555-1234.

What should I do if I lost my boarding pass?

If you lost your boarding pass, contact your airline immediately. They can issue a new one, usually at the check-in counter.
